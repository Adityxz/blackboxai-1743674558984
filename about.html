Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Padépokan Kreatip began as a small collective of designers and developers passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences.

Today, we've grown into a full-service creative agency with a team of 20+ specialists across design, development, and marketing.

Our name "Padépokan Kreatip" reflects our Javanese roots and commitment to being a creative sanctuary where ideas flourish.
